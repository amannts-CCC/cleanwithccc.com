About us

Conscientious Cleaning Co. is a small, locally owned, family-operated cleaning company proudly serving the Cincinnati area. As born-and-raised Cincinnatians with a combined 20+ years of hands-on cleaning experience, we understand the value of reliability, thoroughness, and genuine care.


We take pride in being detail-oriented, dependable, and easy to work with. Our focus is on doing the job right, treating your home with care, and always being responsive when you need us. Whether you’re moving, updating your space, or just trying to keep life running smoothly, we’re here to help make things a little easier—one clean home at a time.


At Conscientious Cleaning Co., you’re not just another appointment—you’re our neighbor. And we’re committed to delivering a cleaning experience that feels personal, polished, and truly reliable.
